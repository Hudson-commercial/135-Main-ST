Description


135 N Chestnut St, New Paltz, NY 12561 is a Retail/warehouse conveniently located in the town of New Paltz NY. The building comes with a newly built warehouse space addition with high ceilings. The property is located in Ulster County and has 1.42 Acres of land and 160 feet of frontage. The uses for this building include storage, warehouse, distribution and other. The property can even be converted to apartments if desired. The building size is 4,598 plus a 2,242 SF Addition 6,840 SF total.
